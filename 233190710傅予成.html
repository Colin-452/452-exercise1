Earthquakes

Data from 100 recent earthquakes in New Zealand were
tabulated to explore the relationship between the depth of the
earthquakes and the magnitude of the earthquakes.

Magnitude
Depth less than 2 betw 2 & 3 betw 3 & 4
0-100km 31 40 5
100-200km 0 13 2
200-300km 0 6 1
300-400km 0 0 2

This table suggests that stronger earthquakes only occur at
greater depths.
